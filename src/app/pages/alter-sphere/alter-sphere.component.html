Story

For its inhabitants, it has always been their own world, but for its many Visitors, they eventually called this world the Alter Sphere

The Alter Sphere "invited" Visitors humans and mythical beasts alike.

The world was kind to the humans, mild to most beasts, but cruel to stronger beasts as their powers are sealed in the Alter Tablets, putting them under the control of their human counterparts.

But time has stopped for them all equally, with no rhyme or reason, no rule or ruler.

Perhaps it is an error of this world or a new test for its inhabitants in their newfound peace.

Dear young Visitor...

With eternity in your hand, will you choose the path of "Coexistence" or trample your hosts in a "Conquest"?
