Tournament Rules

  1. The tournament format is Free Battle
    This means you can battle against any player that are currently free
    You may join or leave at your convenience during the tournament period
  2. No prior registration is needed, but make sure you report your result to the provided form
  3. Submission of result beyond 15.15 CEST will not be processed
  4. You may change parts or the entirety of your deck between matches
  5. For use of Rebirth cards, you may use printed proxy
    *The proxy could only act as a replacement for the official checklist cards, as such it need to be switched with the original card from outside your deck when played
    *You need to inform your opponents prior to the match and use table shuffling in your last shuffle
