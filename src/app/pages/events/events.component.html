Tournament Rules

  1. The tournament format is Swiss round
    Each round will be played in a best-of-1 format
    The time limit for each round is 40 minutes
    The next round may start earlier if all players have finished their rounds and are ready for the next round
    In case of a timeout, the official Battle Spirits Tie Breaker rule would be applied (you can check the details here)
    as such, please notify the @mods if you need to leave briefly (such as to find lunch or toilet)
  2. If you need any assistance, please contact the mods by mentioning @mods in the #tournament-lobby, we will try to assist you as soon as possible
  3. Prior RSVP is needed to join the tournament
    You can do so by purchasing the tournament ticket TBA
    Please create a CardboardCollectible account when purchasing the ticket to ease prize distribution after the event
    Please include your Team Name and your Team Members' Discord Tag in the Checkout Note to ease the registration process
    Registration is closed on February 11th 19:00 (CET)
  4. Please make sure you can position your camera to cover your whole field (You can use the following photo as a reference)
  5. Make sure that cards your hand and deck is always visible on camera
  6. You are required to use table shuffling in your last shuffle
  7. You may spectate other tables when your match is done. However, make sure that your microphone is muted.
  8. You may not make any changes to your deck during the tournament
