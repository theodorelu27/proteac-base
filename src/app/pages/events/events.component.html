Tournament Rules

  1. The tournament format is Free Battle
    This means anyone can battle against anyone in anytime during the tournament period
  2. If you need any assistance, please contact the mods by mentioning @@mods in the #lobby, we will try to assist you as soon as possible
  3. No prior registration is needed, but make sure you report your result to the provided form
  4. Please make sure you can position your camera to cover your whole field
  5. Submission of result beyond 17.15 CEST will not be processed
  6. You may change parts or the entirety of your deck between matches
  7. For use of Rebirth cards, you may use printed proxy
    *The proxy could only act as a replacement for the official checklist cards, as such it need to be switched with the original card from outside your deck when played
    *You need to inform your opponents prior to the match and use table shuffling in your last shuffle

Tournament Reporting

  1. Only one player need to report for each game, do take note to input the winner of the game in the first field.
  2. Games are reported in a best-of-1 basis, each game wins are worth 3 points
  3. Please make sure you use the same name when registering results to maintain the integrity of the Leaderboard
  4. When playing against opponent with multiple devices, please confirm with your opponent on which they want to be registered with
