Deck Construction Regulation

In addition to the official deck construction regulations, all teams are required to adhere to the following rules:
  1. Each team can only bring a total of 3 cards with the same name.
    Only 1 card from the "Restricted to 1 List can be used per team.
    Only 20 cards from the "Restricted to 20 List can be used per team.
  2. You will need to submit your deck list to the following form after purchasing your ticket here

Tournament Rules

  1. The tournament format is Swiss round
    Each round will be played in a best-of-1 format
    The time limit for each round is 40 minutes
    The next round may start earlier if all players have finished their rounds and are ready for the next round
    In case of a timeout, the official Battle Spirits Tie Breaker rule would be applied (you can check the details here)
    as such, please notify the @mods if you need to leave briefly (such as to find lunch or toilet)
  2. If you need any assistance, please contact the mods by mentioning @mods in the #tournament-lobby, we will try to assist you as soon as possible
  3. Prior RSVP is needed to join the tournament
    You can do so by purchasing the tournament ticket here
    Please create a CardboardCollectible account when purchasing the ticket to ease prize distribution after the event
    Please include your Team Name and your Team Members' Discord Tag in the Checkout Note to ease the registration process
    Registration is closed on 22 October 13:00 (CEST)
    *The lists will be checked by a member of the moderator team that might join the tournament. However, please rest assured that an NDA is in place.
  4. Special regulation for Teams with 2 or less players
    While ideally you would be expected to be joining with 3 members on your team, to accomodate newer members and starting teams, we are testing a special regulation for teams with 2 or less members
    For teams with 2 members
    - Each member are required to prepare 2 decks, all 4 decks need to follow the Team Construction Regulation
    - During deck an play order regulation, fill only Player A and Player B, Player C can be filled with "N/A"
    - Both members would be required to play in Table A and B of their respective rounds first, the player the finished their game first can proceed to their Table C game
    - When naming your deck list files, please indicate which deck you would use on your Table C game, decks registered for Table C game can only be used during Table C game
    For teams with 1 member
    - You are required to prepare 3 decks, all 3 decks need to follow the Team Construction Regulation
    - During deck an play order regulation, fill Player A B C with the general concept of your deck (e.g.: Grow / Green Ramp / White Control)
    - You would be required to play in Table A first, and move to the next tables in order after you finished your game
    - You would need to use the same deck for each table (e.g.: If you choose your deck A for Table A, you would need to play all Table A games with deck A)
    There will be no time extension and price/prize reduction for team with 2 or less members
  5. Please make sure you can position your camera to cover your whole field (You can use the following photo as a reference)
  6. Make sure that cards in your hand and deck is always visible on camera
  7. You are required to use table shuffling in your last shuffle
  8. You may spectate other tables when your match is done. However, make sure that your microphone is muted.
  9. You may not make any changes to your deck during the tournament
  10. For use of Rebirth cards, you may use printed proxy
    *The proxy could only act as a replacement for the official checklist cards, as such it need to be switched with the original card from outside your deck when played
